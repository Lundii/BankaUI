Why choose us

Banka is a light-weight core banking application that powers basic banking operations. It allows an user to sign up, create a bank account, deposit into the account, withdraw from the account, transfer funds and make airtime recharge. Founded in 2012, Banka has served over 2000 customers, processed more than a million transactions and opened numerous outlet so you can easily credit or debit your account.
